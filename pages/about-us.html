Daniel Toloza

Professional Dancer in Argentine Tango and Folklore
Daniel Toloza, originally from Córdoba, Argentina, is a renowned dancer specializing in Argentine tango
and folklore, with extensive experience in Latin, Hip-Hop, and other dance styles. He currently
resides between Buenos Aires and travels internationally for performances. Daniel has worked with prestigious
companies such as Tango Pasión, Baires Tango, and Tango Mio, and has participated in renowned
productions like An Argentine in Paris and El Viejo Almacen. He has also performed with the Orquesta
Banda Sinfónica de Buenos Aires and participated in major festivals like La Falda and Cosquín.
Notable Achievements:
First place in two categories at the Ambassador's Tango Cup London 2023.
Finalist at the Metropolitano Buenos Aires 2022.
Performances at prestigious theaters and milongas in Argentina, Brazil, and Italy.
Lead dancer in productions like La Falda and Cia José Catro.
Current Roles:
Organizer (February 2024 - present): Tango practices at Sumate Mondays, London.
Guest Teacher (September 2023 - present): Group classes at various milongas and practices in London.
DJ (September 2023 - present): At milongas like Corrientes Milonga and D'Art Milonga, London.
With his dedication and talent, Daniel continues to make a mark in the tango world, sharing his passion for dance on international stages.

Goldie Teoh

Professional Tango Dancer
Goldie Teoh began her tango journey in London and soon expanded her knowledge of the dance by
training in Buenos Aires. In London, she studies with Mina and Diomar Giraldo Escobar, renowned for
their work with the Corrientes Tango school and their choreography on BBC's Strictly Come Dancing.
During her visits to Buenos Aires, Goldie continues to learn from her mentor, Mabel Alvarez, gaining
deep insights into the culture and community of tango.
Goldie now collaborates with Daniel Toloza on various projects, including their weekly Sumate Mondays
Practilonga and performances at Gaucho, a famous restaurant chain in Covent Garden, London.
Additionally, she is a guest teacher at several tango classes across London.
Notable Achievement:
First place in two categories at the Ambassador's Tango Cup London 2023 with Daniel Toloza.
Current Roles:
Organizer (February 2024 - present): Tango practices at Sumate Mondays, London.
Guest Teacher (September 2023 - present): Group classes at various milongas across London.
Dinner Show Performer (December 2023 - present): Performing at Gaucho restaurant, Covent Garden, London.
Goldie continues to thrive in the London tango scene, sharing her passion and expertise with dancers around the world.
